Templates

the shapes the machine has learned — read off examples, not shipped ⟵ Reader

Each template is how a kind is built — learned by reading good examples and letting the structure emerge (the core engine finds the line/stanza cut from the examples' own surprise). The learner reads both halves: the form (lines, meter, terminators) and the matter (the recurring vocabulary, the stock phrases, what each section keeps being about). Nothing kind-specific is shipped; the only built-in shape is the universal arc. Learned shapes persist in this browser (localStorage), the way the CLI persists them to the templates/ folder.
