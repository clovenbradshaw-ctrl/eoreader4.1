⛁ The reading, as JSONL

Every ingested document is read through the whole predictive stack — the γ-mass prediction over who acts next, the two surprise channels (novelty and Bayesian belief-shift), the connectivity bridge, the turning-point spine, and the enacted frame loop. This surface streams that read as newline-delimited JSON: a head, the extracted structure (round-trippable EoT), a turn per turning point, and a rec where the reading's frame broke. No model, no weights — it runs on the mechanical read.
