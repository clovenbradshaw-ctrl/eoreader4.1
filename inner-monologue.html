☾ The inner monologue, at rest

The chatbot posture is inert until prompted — but idleness is a gate held shut, not the machine's nature. When the model is not otherwise busy, the reading turns back on the document it already holds: it surfs to the place of most interest (the surprising portions), folds the content there, and has a reflection about it. Each reflection is stored into the graph as significance-level content — held at band void, reafferent (canWitness false), so it enriches the reading but can never be mistaken for a witnessed fact. No model, no weights — the note is the significance read voiced. Press Let it rest and watch it think.
